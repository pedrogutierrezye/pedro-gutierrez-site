About Me

I’m Pedro Gutierrez, a multilingual finance executive with over 15 years of global experience across Europe, the U.S., and Latin America. I specialize in driving business transformation, ERP integration, and cash flow optimization to help companies achieve strategic clarity and measurable results.

As Finance Project Manager at REPA Group, I’m currently leading a company-wide Net Working Capital initiative that’s improving DSO by 10% and streamlining financial consolidation across 10 European entities into US GAAP via OneStream.

Previously, I held key finance roles in multinational companies including Microsoft, where I supported financial strategy and operations under the CFO of the $14B Server & Tools business group, leading global reporting, budgeting processes, and incentive compensation planning. At Sabre, I led cost restructuring efforts and delivered financial insights for Sales Planning across North America and Latin America.

I bring a rare combination of corporate discipline, startup innovation, and cross-border finance expertise. My leadership approach is grounded in transparency, adaptability, and long-term thinking.
